PurpleOrangeGames We are an indie group of developers and artists that work in our spare time making what we love, games. We are working on several projects, with each one usually being maintained by one member of the group.


Current Projects

StarLife

Turn based 4X Space Game Inspired by Master of Orion and Eador.
Our idea is to have a twist, something different. Instead of a game based on war and multiplayer, it will focus on singleplayer and interactions between species.

FatRabbit

Reverse Minesweeper In FatRabbit you have to find food for your rabbit before it starves to death. With turn based and realtime modes, you will have to be careful with both traps, and opponents who might be as hungry as your rabbit!
